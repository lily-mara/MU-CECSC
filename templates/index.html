Our Mission

The Miami CEC Student Council was founded in 2012 by students enrolled within Miami University's School of Engineering and Applied Science (now College of Engineering and Computing). Dedicated to promoting engineering and uniting students together, the Miami Engineering Council not only advocates the work and research from their peers, but also hosts various campus-wide events.
